Last Updated: October 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At Ulucado, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right. We design our products to protect your privacy and give you control over your information.


1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data generated by our apps stays on your device. We have no access to your habits, your goals, or your usage patterns.


2. Third-Party Services

Our apps are self-contained. We do not use third-party analytics services that track your behavior across other apps and websites.


3. No Accounts Required

You do not need to create an account to use our apps. This means we don't store your email, password, or any other personal identifiers.


4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us.
